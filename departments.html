Our Departments

Departments at Jesham Learning Centre

Security Department

The security department ensures the safety of all students, staff, and visitors. They work diligently to maintain a secure and welcoming environment for learning and growth.

Sports Department

The sports department encourages physical fitness and teamwork through various activities and competitions. Our programs aim to nurture talent and promote a healthy lifestyle among students.

Administration Department

The administration department handles the day-to-day operations of the school. They ensure smooth coordination between all departments and provide essential support for students and staff.

Academics Department

The academics department is dedicated to providing quality education. Our qualified teachers and comprehensive curriculum aim to empower students to achieve their full potential.
